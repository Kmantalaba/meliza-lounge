## 🎨 INSTANT CUSTOMIZATIONS

### Change Your Price
Find: `₱799.00`  
Change to: `₱YOUR_PRICE`

### Change Your Hours
Find: `WORKING_HOURS = ['09:00', '10:00'...]`  
Edit times. Current: **8 AM - 5 PM**

### Change Advance Booking Days
Find: `MIN_ADVANCE_DAYS = 3`  
Change to: `2` or `7` (your preference)

### Change Closed Days
Find: `date.getDay() === 1` (1 = Monday)  
Change number: 0=Sun, 1=Mon, 2=Tue, 3=Wed, etc.

### Change Gold Color
Find: `#DAA520` (appears ~50 times)  
Replace with: Your color code
- Rose: `#E91E63`
- Purple: `#9C27B0`
- Teal: `#00897B`

## 📅 BOOKING LOGIC (How It Works)

```
Customer Books MORNING (8 AM - 1 PM):
→ AFTERNOON (1 PM - 5 PM) available SAME DAY
→ If afternoon also booked, no more slots

Customer Books AFTERNOON (1 PM - 5 PM):
→ Morning NOT available same day
→ Must book NEXT AVAILABLE DAY

Always Required:
→ Book 3+ days in advance
→ Cannot book on Monday
→ Can only book 1 slot per day per customer
```
